# Comparative Demography: Maharashtra vs Uttar Pradesh

## Overview

This project is a group academic study involving a comparative demographic analysis of Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh using survey-based data. The study examines fertility patterns, infant mortality, and socio-economic disparities across the two states.

The objective is to understand how demographic and socio-economic factors influence population outcomes and regional inequality in India.

---

## Project Year: 2025

## Objectives

* Compare fertility patterns across Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh
* Analyze infant mortality and its socio-economic determinants
* Study spatial inequality in income distribution
* Evaluate demographic differences using statistical modeling

---

## Data

The analysis is based on survey and demographic datasets containing:

* Fertility and childbirth history
* Infant mortality indicators
* Household income and wealth indices
* Education levels
* Contraception usage
* Regional and district-level variation

---

## Methodology

### Regression Modeling

* Poisson survey regression for fertility analysis
* Inclusion of socio-economic covariates such as education, wealth, and contraception use

### Hypothesis Testing

* Testing residence effects on fertility patterns
* Statistical comparison across states and regions

### Demographic Analysis

* Early childbirth (first birth before age 20) analysis
* Infant mortality and postnatal feeding practices
* Spatial inequality in income distribution
